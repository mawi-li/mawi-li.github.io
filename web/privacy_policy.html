Privacy Policy – mawi.li

At mawi.li, we respect your privacy and explain how we handle information during your visit. This policy covers the website sections, software downloads, and cookies.

web

Main Website Policy

I don't collect any information about you except what you willingly provide.

When you send a message via the contact form, I'll ask for:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Subject
  • Message content

When you purchase a product or request a service, I only need the necessary information to contact you and complete your request.

There is no user login, but we collect some technical information to improve your experience.

Technical Information We Collect:

  • Site performance optimization without using your location
  • Your browser's preferred language to automatically display content in the appropriate language
  • This information is used solely for technical performance improvement and user experience

Note: We do not store or share this information with third parties, and it is only used during your visit to the website.
